# Subscription and Billing

Owners can access the the Subscription page by clicking the gear icon in the upper-right corner of your workspace. From there, select **Subscription**.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (591).png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Accessing the workspace Subscription page</p></figcaption></figure>

Once on the Subscription page, you can compare plans and [reach out to us](../getting-started/reach-out-to-us.md) about plans customized to meet your needs. &#x20;
